    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply add the dishes you want to your cart and click the “Checkout via WhatsApp” button. Your order details including items, quantities, and total will automatically appear in a WhatsApp message. Provide your delivery location and let us know if you’ll pay via bank transfer or cash on delivery. We’ll confirm your order and prepare it for delivery.

    Do you accept cash on delivery?

    No, all payments must be completed before your food is delivered. The good news is delivery is completely free. Once you place your order via WhatsApp, we’ll share secure payment details so you can complete your payment before we bring your meal.

    What areas do you deliver to?

    For now, we deliver to selected areas in Texas including Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio, and Fort Worth. Delivery is always fast and free!

    Can I customize my food?

    Absolutely! After placing your order via WhatsApp, you can tell us exactly how you want your food from spice level to portion size, or even adding extra ingredients. We’ll prepare it just the way you like.
